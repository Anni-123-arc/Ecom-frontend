Getting Started FAQs

  • Do I need an account to shop?
    You can browse without logging in, but to buy, you need an account.
  • Is registration free?
    Yes, creating an account is completely free.
  • I forgot my password. What now?
    Click “Forgot Password” on the login page to reset it.
  • Why does it say my account doesn't exist?
    Make sure you registered with the same email you are trying to log in with, or sign up if you’re new.
  • Is my data safe here?
    Yes, we use encryption to protect your information.
  • Can I use the site on mobile?
    Absolutely, our site is mobile-friendly.
